How am I paid?

There is no mystery in how compensation works. Here is what you should know before making any decision.

01 · Carrier-paid commission

If you choose a product, the issuing insurance carrier pays the commission.

02 · No added cost to you

Working with Arth Path Financial does not add a separate fee to the product you select.

03 · Clear disclosure

Compensation and material product details can be reviewed before an application is signed.

04 · Best-interest standard

Recommendations are made under the applicable insurance best-interest standard.

05 · No pressure to proceed

You decide whether any option is right for you. A conversation never obligates you to buy.
