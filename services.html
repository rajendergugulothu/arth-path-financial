What we do

We look at your entire financial life. Not just your investments.

Your investments affect your taxes. Your insurance protects your income. Your estate planning protects your family. These things are connected and they need to be looked at together.

01

Financial Planning

A written plan that maps your income, your assets, your goals and your timeline. Not a one-time document. Something we revisit with you every year as your life changes.

02

Insurance and Protection

We review what you have and tell you what you actually need. Most families are either underinsured on term life or paying for expensive whole life they were sold. We sort that out honestly.

04

Legacy and Estate

Wills, beneficiary designations, trusts, powers of attorney. Making sure that when the time comes, your wealth goes to the right people without probate delays, family disputes or unnecessary estate tax.

05

Retirement Planning

401(k), IRA, Roth conversions, Social Security timing. We help you figure out exactly how much you need, when you can realistically retire, and what to do with old employer accounts sitting idle.

06

Tax Planning

We do not prepare your taxes but we work alongside your CPA to make sure your investment decisions and withdrawals are structured in a tax-efficient way throughout the year, not just in April.
