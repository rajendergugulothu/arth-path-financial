Planning Areas

Four goals. One independent advisor working all four together.

Retirement, your kids' education, your tax exposure and the coverage that protects all of it — planned as one picture, not four separate purchases.

01
💵

Retirement Planning

Guaranteed income sources — fixed and fixed-indexed annuities, Social Security timing and pension elections — structured so a paycheck-free life still has a paycheck. We map what you have saved against what you'll actually spend, then close the gap.

02
🎓

Kids' Education Planning

Funding strategies for college and beyond, weighed against your retirement timeline so one goal doesn't quietly cannibalize the other. We look at the tradeoffs honestly before anything is committed.

03
🧾

Tax-Efficient Strategy

Structuring retirement income, life insurance and annuity withdrawals to manage tax exposure over time — coordinated with your CPA, not in place of one. The goal is keeping more of what you've built, across the whole timeline.

04
🛡️

Life Insurance

Term, whole and indexed universal life, compared across carriers for the death benefit, cash value growth and guarantees that match your family's actual needs — never more coverage, or a more expensive type, than the situation calls for.

05
🏥

Medicare & Health Planning

Medicare Advantage, Supplement and Part D comparisons during your enrollment window, plus ongoing review as plans and needs change each year. We explain the differences in plain language so you can choose with confidence.

06
🏛️

Long-Term Care & Legacy

Standalone LTC, hybrid life/LTC and beneficiary structuring that complements — not replaces — your estate attorney's work, so the people you love are protected and the handoff is clean.

New Client Workflow

What actually happens after you reach out.

Five steps from first contact to an active, reviewed plan — no step skipped, nothing signed before you've seen the comparison.

01

Initial Conversation

A free, no-obligation call to understand your situation — income, savings, dependents, health coverage and what you're trying to solve for.

02

Fact-Finding & Goals

A structured intake covering retirement timeline, education needs, existing coverage and tax situation, so the plan is built on your full picture.

03

Independent Comparison

I compare appropriate options for your situation and bring back clear product comparisons — costs, guarantees and tradeoffs.

04

Recommendation & Paperwork

We review the options together in writing. Once you decide, I handle the application and stay the point of contact through underwriting.

05

Onboarding & Review

Once your policy is in force, I confirm you understand how it works, then check in annually or whenever circumstances change.
